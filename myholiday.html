My Holiday Experience

Written by: Karenina Kore Ludji - 2382012

Hello, my name is Karenina. I want to tell you about some events during this holiday. I went back to my home in Kupang, NTT. During the holiday, I had to take care of my niece and work at the family's shop. Each morning began with preparing breakfast for my niece before rushing to the shop. She often played around while I served customers, making it an exciting yet tiring challenge. 🎉👶

At the shop, I tried to keep everything organized and customers satisfied. My niece enjoyed helping with small tasks, though sometimes creating a bit of chaos. Despite being busy, we often chatted and joked, making workdays more enjoyable and the holiday moments unforgettable. 🛍💬

As evening came and the shop closed, I returned home to spend time with my niece. We often watched movies or read books together before bed. This holiday provided a valuable opportunity to strengthen our bond while managing responsibilities at the shop, making it a meaningful experience. 📖🍿
